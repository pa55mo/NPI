About Us

Nerys Powles, hailing from the vibrant town of Merthyr Tydfil in South Wales, left the church of the latter day saints' strict traditions behind at thirteen. Fueled by a desire for change, she founded Nerys Powles Industries at thirty-seven, a company dedicated to global impact. Her innovative solutions in sustainability and community empowerment have made her a beacon of inspiration, proving that resilience and determination know no boundaries.
